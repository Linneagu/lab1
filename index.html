About me

Hi! My name is Linnea, I'm 29 years old and currently living in Kalmar while studying to become an Interaction Designer. I'm passionate about creating intuitive, meaningful user experiences and enjoy exploring how design can make everyday interactions smoother and more engaging. I'm a curious and creative person who loves solving problems through thoughtful design. My strengths lie in understanding user needs, paying attention to details, and turning complex ideas into clear and intuitive solutions. I enjoy combining creativity with structure, and I'm always looking for new ways to grow and improve as a designer.
